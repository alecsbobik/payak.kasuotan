Logo

The bow that forms to be the letter P representing clothes. This bow means not being afraid to stand out in the crowd.

Logotype

Payák is a tagalog term for "Simple". Its clothes are inspired by the simplicity and comfortable way Filipino society dresses up in instances.

Combination of Logo and Logotype

The visual of logo combined with the logotype.

Logotype with tagline

Payák is hard to identify, so adding another word to make it easily identify is to add "Kasuotan" a tagalog word for clothing. Now in just one read a person can identify the brand that it is a clothing line.

Core Brand Colors

Here’s the common hierarchy of our core colors in which they’re used:

#A0ED6D represents refreshing, peace, and calm.

#000000 represents elegance, and formality look.

#FFFFFF represents a sense of simpleness and cleanliness.
